=== 04 MOTION ===
Three durations. Two easings. One pulse.

  --dur-fast    120ms · hover micro
  --dur-med     200ms · panel / tab
  --dur-slow    360ms · result reveal
  --ease-out    cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)
  --ease-in-out cubic-bezier(0.65, 0, 0.35, 1)

@keyframes pulse  1.6s ease-in-out infinite · live indicator

=== 05 GRADIENT SWEEP ===
Applied via background-clip: text. Static — never animated. Reserved for "autonomous work".

  stop · 0%    #5EE3F5
  stop · 55%   #A78BFA
  stop · 100%  #F472B6

  --grad-sweep: linear-gradient(90deg, #5EE3F5 0%, #A78BFA 55%, #F472B6 100%);
